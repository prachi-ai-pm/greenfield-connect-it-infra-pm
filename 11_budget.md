## Budget Lifecycle

This budget moved through three stages: the **original charter budget** (Stage 1 approval), a **revised budget** (Stage 2 variance request, driven by the cabling contractor's certified site survey finding ~20% more cable runs than the floor plan assumed), and the **final actual** (incorporating the Stage 4 conference-room change request).

## Notes on This Budget
- The **contingency reserve was intentionally protected**: of ₹4.5L set aside, the project drew on it minimally — most risks (WAN lead time, electrical permit delay, contractor under-staffing) were managed through schedule re-sequencing and contractual remedies rather than additional spend. This is worth calling out explicitly in an interview: a healthy contingency reserve that goes mostly unused is itself a sign of good risk management, not wasted budget.
- Every variance in this budget has a documented approval trail (sponsor sign-off or formal change request) — none was absorbed informally, consistent with the governance approach in the [Communication Plan](./10-communication-plan.md).
